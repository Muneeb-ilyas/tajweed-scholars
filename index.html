About Tajweed Scholars

Tajweed Scholars is a global platform dedicated to teaching the Holy Quran. We bridge the gap between eager learners worldwide and expert tutors.

Whether you are a beginner taking your first steps in reading Arabic, or an advanced student seeking to perfect your Tajweed and memorize the Quran, our methodology ensures you achieve your goals.

Vision, Mission, and Objectives

Our Vision

To be the leading global platform for online Quranic education, making the authentic learning of the Holy Quran accessible to every household worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, personalized, and engaging Quran classes through certified scholars, utilizing modern technology to foster a deep connection with the Quran.

Our Objectives

Empower students to read with perfect Tajweed, facilitate seamless memorization, and instill a lifelong love for Islamic values and continuous learning.
